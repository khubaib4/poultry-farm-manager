ALERT TYPES AND PRIORITY:

| Type | Priority | Color | Icon | Condition |
|------|----------|-------|------|-----------|
| Overdue | Critical | Red | alert-circle | Past due date, balance > 0 |
| Due Today | High | Orange | clock | Due today, balance > 0 |
| Due Tomorrow | Warning | Yellow | calendar | Due tomorrow, balance > 0 |
| Due This Week | Info | Blue | info | Due within 7 days, balance > 0 |

ALERT DATA STRUCTURE:
```typescript
interface PaymentAlert {
  type: 'overdue' | 'due_today' | 'due_soon';
  priority: 'critical' | 'high' | 'warning' | 'info';
  saleId: number;
  invoiceNumber: string;
  customerId: number;
  customerName: string;
  amount: number;
  balanceDue: number;
  dueDate: string;
  daysOverdue?: number;
  daysTillDue?: number;
}
```

ALERT MESSAGE FORMATS:
- Overdue: "{Customer} - Invoice {#} overdue by {X} days (PKR {amount})"
- Due Today: "{Customer} - Invoice {#} due today (PKR {amount})"
- Due Soon: "{Customer} - Invoice {#} due in {X} days (PKR {amount})"
