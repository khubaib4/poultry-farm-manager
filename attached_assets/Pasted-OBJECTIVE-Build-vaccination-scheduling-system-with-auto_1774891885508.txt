OBJECTIVE: Build vaccination scheduling system with automatic reminders based on flock age

REQUIREMENTS:
- Define vaccination schedule templates (vaccines by flock age)
- Auto-generate vaccination schedule when flock is created
- Show upcoming vaccinations on dashboard
- Vaccination reminders/alerts
- Mark vaccinations as completed or skipped

FILES TO CREATE/MODIFY:

1. src/pages/farm/VaccinationSchedulePage.tsx
   - Route: /farm/vaccinations
   - Header: "Vaccination Management"
   - Tabs: "Upcoming" | "Completed" | "Schedule Template"
   
   - Upcoming Tab:
     * List of vaccinations due in next 30 days
     * Grouped by flock
     * Show: vaccine name, flock name, due date, flock age at due date
     * Actions: Mark Complete, Skip, Reschedule
     * Filter by flock
   
   - Completed Tab:
     * History of administered vaccinations
     * Show: vaccine, flock, scheduled date, actual date, administered by
     * Filter by date range, flock
   
   - Schedule Template Tab:
     * Default vaccination schedule for new flocks
     * Table: Vaccine Name, Age (days), Route, Mandatory, Notes
     * Add/Edit/Delete vaccines in template
     * This is used when creating new flocks

     * Mark as completed

   - vaccinations.skip(vaccinationId, data):
     * Mark as skipped
     * Record reason, notes
     * Optionally reschedule to new date
   
   - vaccinations.reschedule(vaccinationId, newDate):
     * Change scheduled_date
   
   - vaccinationSchedule.getTemplate(farmId):
     * Get farm's vaccination template

   - vaccinationSchedule.addToTemplate(farmId, data):
     * Add vaccine to template
   
   - vaccinationSchedule.updateTemplate(templateId, data):
     * Update vaccine in template
   
   - vaccinationSchedule.removeFromTemplate(templateId):
     * Remove vaccine from template
   
   - vaccinationSchedule.resetToDefaults(farmId):
     * Reset template to standard layer schedule
   
   - vaccinationSchedule.generateForFlock(flockId):
     * Generate vaccinations for flock based on template

CALCULATION FOR DUE DATE:
- Flock current age = (today - arrival_date) + age_at_arrival_days
- Vaccination due date = arrival_date + (vaccine.age_days - age_at_arrival_days)
- Days until due = due_date - today

UI/UX REQUIREMENTS:

- Quick actions to complete/skip
- Calendar view option (future enhancement)
- Mobile-friendly cards

OUTPUT: Vaccination schedule is auto-generated for new flocks. Upcoming vaccinations appear on dashboard and alerts. Users can complete, skip, or reschedule vaccinations.
