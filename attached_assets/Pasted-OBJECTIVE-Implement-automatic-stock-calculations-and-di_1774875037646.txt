CALCULATION FORMULAS:
- Total Birds = SUM(current_count) from active flocks
- Production Rate = (total_eggs / (total_birds × days)) × 100
- Mortality Rate = (total_deaths / initial_total_birds) × 100
- FCR = total_feed_kg / total_eggs
- Trend = compare current week vs previous week

PERFORMANCE THRESHOLDS (for layers):
| Metric | Good | Warning | Critical |
|--------|------|---------|----------|
| Production Rate | >85% | 70-85% | <70% |
| Daily Mortality | <0.1% | 0.1-0.3% | >0.3% |
| Cumulative Mortality | <3% | 3-5% | >5% |
| FCR | <2.0 | 2.0-2.3 | >2.3 |
