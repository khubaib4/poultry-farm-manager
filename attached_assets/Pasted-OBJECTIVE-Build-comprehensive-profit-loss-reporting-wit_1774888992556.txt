CALCULATION FORMULAS:
- Net Profit = Total Revenue - Total Expenses
- Profit Margin = (Net Profit / Total Revenue) × 100
- Revenue Per Bird = Total Revenue / Average Bird Count
- Cost Per Egg = Total Expenses / Total Eggs Produced

- Mobile responsive
