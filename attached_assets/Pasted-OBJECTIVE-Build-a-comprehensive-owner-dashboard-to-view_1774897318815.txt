PERFORMANCE INDICATORS:
- Good (Green): Production >85%, Mortality <0.5%, Profit margin >20%
- Warning (Yellow): Production 70-85%, Mortality 0.5-1%, Profit margin 10-20%
- Critical (Red): Production <70%, Mortality >1%, Profit margin <10%

COMPARISON METRICS:
